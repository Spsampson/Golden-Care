About Us

Golden Care Medical Clinic was founded in 2014 by Medical Director/Chief Physician and Cardiologist, Dr. Ricky Maharaj. As a result of his hard work and dedication, he stood out of the crowd and gained the trust of many clients and his organization has been booming ever since. Here at Golden Care Medical, our main objective is to provide our customers with the best customer services in a warm yet professional environment. Our staff is trained and equipped with the necessary skills to deliver quality care while being proactive in implementing our procedures and standards of excellence. Golden Care Medical pledges to: Provide care that is golden, to be sensitive to the needs of those around us to lift the spirits of the sick and encourage the discouraged. to become someone’s MIRACLE by showing compassion, kindness and empathy. We also strive to be the top go-to medical institution in the health sector by easing our customers of the stress and hassle of dealing with public healthcare facilities. With our diverse services, our organization is considered as a one-stop-shop to facilitate your every need. Available also are discounts on lab work, specials for the elderly every Sunday as well as discounts for those interested in filling their prescriptions for the long haul(3mths +) As the digital platform is constantly changing and taking over the world by storm, we at Golden Care Medical are also evolving in mastering new skills to meet the needs of our new and existing customers with an ecommerce solution. It is evident that nowadays people are joining social media seeking the best and possible health care options. This is why we took the initiative to create a webpage to give our customers a clear incite of what we do so that they can be properly informed in order to make a sound decision. As Brian Tracy said, “There are no limits to what you can accomplish except the limit you place on your own thinking.” Having said that, we hope to reach as many people as possible resulting in not only more recognition for our organization but also more satisfied customers. So feel free to explore our page and if any questions/queries should arise, we are on standby to tend to your needs. Have a great day and we look forward to hearing from you.
