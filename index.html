About Us

Golden Care Medical Clinic was founded in 2014 by Medical Director/Chief Physician and Cardiologist, Dr. Ricky Maharaj. As a result of his hard work and dedication, he stood out of the crowd and gained the trust of many clients and his organization has been booming ever since. Here at Golden Care Medical, our main objective is to provide our customers with the best customer services in a warm yet professional environment. Our staff is trained and equipped with the necessary skills to deliver quality care while being proactive in implementing our procedures and standards of excellence. .
